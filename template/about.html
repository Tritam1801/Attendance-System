About

This is an attendance system, which takes a csv file having student present in class with the total time of their presence and marks their presence in another sheet based on the present percentage specified by the teacher.

In the form a teacher has to specify the semester, input file, output file, and the present percentage and submit it to successfully mark the attendance

This is developed by Tritam Mohanty, a final year student of BSc. (Hons.) Data Science, Sri Sri University, Odisha, India.
